How Our AI Credit Scoring Works

Traditional credit scoring often overlooks the unique needs and circumstances of small and medium enterprises. ABSA's AI-powered credit scoring system takes a more holistic approach, analyzing multiple data points to give your business a fair assessment.

Our AI Credit Scoring Process

1

Data Collection

We securely collect relevant financial data from your business through our digital platform. This includes bank statements, financial records, and business performance metrics.

The process is automated and secure, requiring minimal manual input from you.

2

AI Analysis

Our sophisticated AI algorithm analyzes multiple data points, looking beyond traditional credit scores to understand your business's true financial health.

The system identifies patterns, trends, and potential in ways that conventional methods cannot.

3

Score Generation & Offer

Based on the comprehensive analysis, we generate a proprietary ABSA SME Score that reflects your business's creditworthiness more accurately.

This score helps determine suitable loan products, amounts, and terms tailored specifically to your business needs.
