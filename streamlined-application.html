Is Your Business Eligible?

See if your SME meets our basic criteria for a streamlined loan application:

Registered Business

Your business must be officially registered and operating within South Africa.

Minimum Operating Period

Your business should have been operational for at least 6 months (adjust as needed).

Minimum Annual Revenue

Your business should have a minimum annual revenue of R100,000 (adjust as needed).

Good Business Standing

Your business should have a good financial standing without major defaults.
